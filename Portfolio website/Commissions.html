Commissions




Types of Commissions



These are the types of Commissions I do.


Character Design





Character design commissions involve creating distinctive, visually appealing characters based on a client's vision. The process involves gathering references, discussing personality, setting, and design preferences, and developing original concepts. Final deliverables may include illustrations, busts, expression sheets, and outfit variations.

Portraits





Portrait commissions capture personality, emotion, and likeness through careful composition and detail. They include head and shoulders illustrations, ideal for profile images, character sheets, promotional materials, or personal keepsakes. Each portrait is rendered with attention to form, anatomy, and visual impact. These could be digital or traditional.



Custom wallpaper





Custom wallpaper commissions offer personalized illustrations for digital spaces, based on aesthetic preferences, themes, or original concepts. The process includes concept development, layout planning, and stylistic customization, ensuring functional and visually impactful high-resolution images.
