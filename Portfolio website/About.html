About Me

Hi! I’m Daniel Falzon, a 19 year old artist currently learning and growing as a graphic designer. Creativity has always been a core part of who I am whether I’m sketching, painting, or experimenting with digital media. As I explore the world of design, I’m focused on blending artistic expression with purposeful visual communication.



My portfolio reflects both my passion for art and my journey into graphic design. I’m inspired by bold ideas, meaningful messages, and the power of visuals to make people think, feel, and connect. Every project is a step toward sharpening my skills and finding my voice as a designer.



Thanks for checking out my work I’m excited to keep learning and creating.
